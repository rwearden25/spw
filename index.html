FAQ

Before you ask.

Do you service restaurants on a recurring schedule?

Yes — the majority of our work is recurring restaurant maintenance (sidewalks, patios, grease pads, drive-thrus). Monthly is the most common cadence; bi-weekly and quarterly are available.

What equipment do you run?

Honda iGX800 belt-drive hot-water unit on a 355-gallon trailer, towed by a 2017 Ram 2500 Cummins. Hot water is what separates restaurant-grade cleans from cold-water surface rinses.

Do you work after-hours?

Yes. Most restaurant cleans happen after close. The pad is dry and the lot is open well before your morning prep crew arrives.

How far do you travel?

Aledo is home base, but routes cover the entire DFW metroplex — from east Dallas to Weatherford, Denton to Cleburne. Multi-location accounts are our specialty.

Can you stripe the lot too?

We partner with SC Paint Striping for re-stripes when the concrete is back to clean — single quote, single schedule.

Are you insured?

Yes — fully insured for commercial work. COI available to corporate facilities teams on request.
